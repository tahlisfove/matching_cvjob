How to Match Resumes to a Job

1

Upload Resumes

In the dedicated section, upload the resumes of the candidates you wish to evaluate. You must upload a ZIP file containing multiple resumes at once, as only one file can be uploaded in this area.

2

Choose the Job Title

Select the relevant IT field for the position. This step enables us to effectively target the skills required for the job.

3

Additional Information

If you have specific criteria or additional information about the skills or qualities you are looking for, you can add them at this stage.

4

Matching

By clicking on the "Matching" button, our AI algorithm compares the candidates' resumes with the job description. You will then receive a list of candidates that best match your criteria.

5

Next

The matching results are displayed in the section on the right. Resumes are sorted by matching score, allowing you to see the most relevant candidates first, with the possibility of downloading the resumes of candidates to review or contact them for an interview.
