Frequently asked questions

Persistent question? Our team is here to help!

What type of resume files are supported?

Currently only PDF files are supported. Ensure that your resume files are converted to PDF format before uploading the .zip folder or the .pdf file on our platform.

How many resume files can I upload at once in a single compressed (ZIP) folder?

There's no limit to the number of resume files you can upload in a single compressed (ZIP) folder, feel free to upload as many as you need.

How does the candidate and job posting matching process work?

After selecting an open position from our list and completing the optional text section, our AI algorithm will analyze information extracted from resume files against the job posting to calculate similarity percentage. This results in a list with the top 8 best potential candidates for the given role.

Is there any limit regarding how many times you may attempt matching?

No, you may initiate the matching process as often as necessary without limitations.

Can I view individual similarity scores for each candidate after matching?

No, only the top 8 candidates with the highest matching percentages are displayed. However, individual similarity scores for each candidate will soon be available.

Is it possible to add a profession to the available selection choices?

No, the list of professions is updated via a government website each month and is specific to each country. Adding is not available. However, if you wish to match with a profession not in the list, choose the "Other" and "Personalised" option, then enter in the Additional Information area the name and full description corresponding to the desired profession.

What additional details can be entered into the text box prior to matching?

The text field allows users to specify important aspects, specific requirements or internal notes related to the open position. These inputs will factor into the matching process.

Regarding privacy and security, how do stored resumes get handled?

We take data privacy and security very seriously, all resume files are kept encrypted on secure servers accessible solely by authorized HR personnel within your organization.

What should one do if unsatisfied with initial matching outcome?

Modify the job description by adding additional information according to the desired candidate profile, then reset the matching process to obtain refined results.

How does MatchifyJobs ensure fairness in the recruitment process?

MatchifyJobs is committed to promoting fairness and inclusion in recruitment. Our algorithm is designed to eliminate potential biases and promote an objective selection of candidates based solely on skills and suitability for the position.

Is it possible to customize search criteria during the candidate matching process?

Yes, our platform offers full flexibility in customizing search criteria. You can define specific criteria such as experience, education, skills, etc., to obtain more precise results.

How can I contact the support team of MatchifyJobs in case of issues or questions?

You can contact our support team via our contact form on our website, or by email at support@matchifyjobs.com.

Which languages are supported on the MatchifyJobs platform?

Currently, MatchifyJobs only supports English. We are also working on adding new languages like French to better serve our users. However, the site is available in both English and French to facilitate greater understanding among users.
